🛠 Installation Steps

1 Install the APK
Install on your Mobile, Google TV, or Firestick. If using TV, use the Downloader method or the "Send Files to TV" app from your phone.
2 Open & Restore
Open TiviMate → Go to Settings → General → Restore Data.
3 Select Backup
Browse your files and select ultimate.tmb. Click Restore.

📺 Playlist Configuration

I have pre-added dummy portals (Stalker / Xtream / M3U). To add your own:

  1. Go to Settings → Playlist.
  2. Select your preferred type (Stalker, Xtream, or M3U).
  3. Go to Parameters → Edit.
  4. Enter your URL, Username, and Password.
  5. Click Apply Changes.
⚠️ Note: Only valid M3U/Stalker/Xtream links will work. If it fails, double-check your credentials and try updating a few times.
