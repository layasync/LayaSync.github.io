📡 WiFi Connection (Samsung TV API)
⚠️ Step 0 — Required one-time setup:
Browsers block connections to local IPs from saved HTML files. You must run a tiny local server first.

On your PC/Mac, open Terminal and run:
python3 -m http.server 8080

Then on your phone open Chrome and go to:
http://YOUR_PC_IP:8080/samsung-remote.html
(find your PC IP in Network Settings)
1
Find TV IP: Samsung TV → Settings → General → Network → Network Status → IP Settings → note the IP address
2
Enable remote access: Samsung TV → Settings → General → External Device Manager → Device Connect Manager → set to First Time Only
3
Enter TV IP below and tap Connect. Watch your TV — a permission popup will appear, accept it.
Port: tries 8002 (WSS) then 8001 (WS) automatically
🔵 Bluetooth
⚠️ Browser Bluetooth cannot control Samsung TVs directly.

Samsung TVs use Bluetooth HID (keyboard/gamepad protocol). Browsers only support GATT/BLE devices (earbuds, fitness trackers etc.) — not HID. So the TV popup never appears via Bluetooth from a browser.

To use Bluetooth control: Pair your phone as a Bluetooth keyboard at the OS level through your phone's Bluetooth settings, then the TV will treat your phone like a keyboard.
1
On Samsung TV: Settings → General → External Device Manager → Input Device Manager → Bluetooth Device List → put TV in pairing mode
2
On your phone: Settings → Bluetooth → scan and pair with your Samsung TV name
3
Once paired at OS level, use the WiFi tab above for the best remote experience (more buttons, voice, apps)
TV likely won't appear here — use OS Bluetooth settings instead
